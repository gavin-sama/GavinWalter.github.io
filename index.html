About Me

My name is Gavin Walter, and I am a BlueSky Student attending East Tennessee State University (ETSU) at BlueCross BlueShield of Tennessee. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computing with a concentration in Information Systems. I began the program in August 2023 and am on track to graduate by December 2025. With a strong foundation in computing, data analytics, and engineering, I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. My current focus includes data cleansing, analytics, and information delivery, particularly in the healthcare industry. Feel free to explore my projects and connect with me!
